## 数据集与基准

### 情感分析

1. **StockEmotions: Discover Investor Emotions for Financial Sentiment Analysis and Multivariate Time Series.** AAAI Bridge, 2023. [论文](https://arxiv.org/abs/2301.09279)

   *Jean Lee, Hoyoul Luis Youn, Josiah Poon, Soyeon Caren Han*
   
   > 1万条StockTwits推文，12种细粒度情绪标注

2. **Trillion Dollar Words: A New Financial Dataset, Task & Market Analysis.** ACL, 2023. [论文](https://arxiv.org/abs/2305.07972)

   *Agam Shah, Suvan Paturi, Sudheer Chava*
   
   > FOMC数据集（1996-2022），鹰派/鸽派立场分类

### 信息抽取

3. **FiNER: Financial Numeric Entity Recognition for XBRL Tagging.** ACL, 2023. [论文](https://arxiv.org/abs/2302.11157)

   *Agam Shah, Ruchit Vithani, Abhinav Gullapalli, Sudheer Chava*
   
   > 首个高质量金融NER数据集，双重人工标注

4. **FinRED: A Dataset for Relation Extraction in Financial Domain.** WWW Workshop, 2022. [论文](https://arxiv.org/abs/2306.03736)

   *Soumya Sharma, Tapas Nayak, Arusarka Bose, Ajay Kumar Meena, Koustuv Dasgupta, Niloy Ganguly, Pawan Goyal*
   
   > 7775句，29种关系类型

### 问答与推理

5. **FinQA: A Dataset of Numerical Reasoning over Financial Data.** EMNLP, 2021. [论文](https://arxiv.org/abs/2109.00122)

   *Zhiyu Chen, Wenhu Chen, Charese Smiley, Sameena Shah, Iana Borova, Dylan Langdon, Reema Moussa, Matt Beane, Ting-Hao Huang, Bryan Routledge, William Yang Wang*
   
   > 首个金融数值推理QA数据集，多步推理准确率仅22.78%

6. **ConvFinQA: Exploring the Chain of Numerical Reasoning in Conversational Finance Question Answering.** EMNLP, 2022. [论文](https://arxiv.org/abs/2210.03849)

   *Zhiyu Chen, Shiyang Li, Charese Smiley, Zhiqiang Ma, Sameena Shah, William Yang Wang*
   
   > FinQA扩展为多轮对话，14115问题

### 摘要生成

7. **ECTSum: A New Benchmark Dataset For Bullet Point Summarization of Long Earnings Call Transcripts.** EMNLP, 2022. [论文](https://arxiv.org/abs/2210.12467) [代码](https://github.com/rajdeep345/ECTSum)

   *Rajdeep Mukherjee, Abhinav Bohra, Akash Banerjee, Soumya Sharma, Manjunath Hegde, Afreen Shaikh, Shivani Shrivastava, Koustuv Dasgupta, Niloy Ganguly, Saptarshi Ghosh, Pawan Goyal*
   
   > 财报电话会议摘要，压缩比>100

### 综合基准

8. **PIXIU: A Large Language Model, Instruction Data and Evaluation Benchmark for Finance.** NeurIPS, 2023. [论文](https://arxiv.org/abs/2306.05443) [代码](https://github.com/The-FinAI/PIXIU)

   *Qianqian Xie, Weiguang Han, Xiao Zhang, Yanzhao Lai, Min Peng, Alejandro Lopez-Lira, Jimin Huang*
   
   > 首个开源金融指令微调模型FinMA + 多任务评测框架

9. **The FinBen: An Holistic Financial Benchmark for Large Language Models.** NeurIPS, 2024. [论文](https://arxiv.org/abs/2402.12659) [代码](https://github.com/The-FinAI/FinBen)

   *Qianqian Xie, Weiguang Han, Zhengyu Chen, Ruoyu Xiang, Xiao Zhang, Yueru He, Mengxi Xiao, Dong Li, Yongfu Dai, Duanyu Feng, et al.*
   
   > 最全面的金融LLM评测：7大类24任务，<70B模型难以稳定遵循交易指令

10. **FinTSB: A Comprehensive and Practical Benchmark for Financial Time Series Forecasting.** arXiv, 2025. [论文](https://arxiv.org/abs/2502.18834)

    *Yifan Hu, Yuante Li, Peiyuan Liu, Yuxia Zhu, Naiqi Li, Tao Dai, Shu-tao Xia, Dawei Cheng, Changjun Jiang*
    
    > 时序预测基准，含真实交易约束（手续费、涨跌停等）

## 金融大模型

1. **BloombergGPT: A Large Language Model for Finance.** arXiv, 2023. [论文](https://arxiv.org/abs/2303.17564)

   *Shijie Wu, Ozan Irsoy, Steven Lu, Vadim Dabravolski, Mark Dredze, Sebastian Gehrmann, Prabhanjan Kambadur, David Rosenberg, Gideon Mann*
   
   > 500亿参数，1:1金融/通用数据混合训练（未开源）

2. **FinTral: A Family of GPT-4 Level Multimodal Financial Large Language Models.** ACL Findings, 2024. [论文](https://arxiv.org/abs/2402.10986)

   *Gagan Bhatia, El Moatez Billah Nagoudi, Hasan Cavusoglu, Muhammad Abdul-Mageed*
   
   > 多模态（文本+表格+图表），7B模型在5/7任务超越GPT-4

3. **FinLLaMA: LLM-based Financial Sentiment Analysis for Algorithmic Trading.** ICAIF, 2024. [论文](https://arxiv.org/abs/2403.12285)

   *Giorgos Iacovides, Thanos Konstantinidis, Mingxue Xu, Danilo Mandic*
   
   > LLaMA-2-7B + LoRA，单卡A100可训，超越FinBERT
